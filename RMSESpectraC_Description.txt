Softare for RMSE comparison between FTIR spectra implemented using sklearn
Fernando Gomes / Biopolymers & Sensors Lab. / IMA - UFRJ
V0.1 - 03/29/21



RMSESpectraC software works with the comparison of FTIR spectra. The comparison is performed between a well-known species (here we used the toluene) versus several polymeric materials (one by one), seeking to establish which of them are more chemically similar to toluene.
